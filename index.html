About Verba

Speaking shouldn't feel like a performance.

Verba was built for Indonesian learners who want to practise without fear — honest, warm feedback every time. Your progress is saved in your browser only. No servers. No tracking.

"The goal isn't a perfect accent. It's a voice that can carry your ideas into any room."

For Indonesian learners, specifically: The AI knows Indonesian speakers often struggle with /v/, /θ/, article use, vowel length, and verb tense — and gives tips that address exactly those patterns.

How AI feedback works: Your speech is transcribed in the browser, then sent to Claude AI which evaluates fluency, grammar, vocabulary, and content at your exact CEFR level — in about 3 seconds.
