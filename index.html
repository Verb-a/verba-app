About Verba

Speaking shouldn't feel like a performance.

Verba was built for Indonesian learners who want to practise without fear — honest, warm feedback every time.

The teaching philosophy

Most learners don't lack knowledge — they lack a safe place to make mistakes. Verba is that place.

Every piece of feedback is designed to build you up. Your level is a starting point, not a ceiling. An A1 learner gets the same care as a C1 learner — just at a different pace.

You don't need to sound like a native speaker. You need to be understood, feel confident, and keep going.

"The goal isn't a perfect accent. It's a voice that can carry your ideas into any room."
— Verba teaching principle
For Indonesian learners, specifically

The AI knows Indonesian speakers often struggle with /v/, /θ/, article use, vowel length, and verb tense — and gives tips that address exactly those patterns.

How the AI works

From your voice to real feedback

🎙️
You speak
Your browser transcribes speech in real time. No audio is stored or uploaded.
🤖
AI analyses
Claude AI evaluates your transcript on fluency, grammar, vocabulary, and content at your exact CEFR level.
💬
You get feedback
Scores, specific praise, gentle suggestions, and an encouragement message — in about 3 seconds.

🔒 Your data stays with you

Verba has no database. Your name, level, and XP are stored in your browser's local storage only.

Clear your browser data and it resets completely. No accounts, no emails, no tracking.
