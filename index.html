About Verba

Speaking shouldn't feel like a performance.

Verba is a warm, judgement-free English speaking app built for Indonesian learners. Real AI feedback from Claude. No server, no tracking — everything stays in your browser.

"The goal isn't a perfect accent. It's a voice that can carry your ideas into any room."

Made for Indonesian learners: The AI knows you may struggle with /v/ vs /b/, the /θ/ sound, articles, and vowel length — and gives tips addressing exactly those patterns.

How feedback works: Your browser transcribes your speech, then Claude AI scores fluency, grammar, vocabulary, and content at your exact CEFR level — in seconds.
